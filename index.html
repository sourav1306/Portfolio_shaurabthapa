Project One

Penetration testing (pen testing) is a controlled and authorized attempt to exploit vulnerabilities in a system, network, or application to evaluate its security strength. Pen testers simulate real-world cyberattacks to identify weaknesses that attackers could use, then provide detailed reports on how to fix them.

Project Two

Ethical hacking is the practice of legally breaking into computer systems to find weaknesses before malicious hackers can exploit them. Ethical hackers use the same tools and techniques as attackers, but with permission and the goal of strengthening security.
